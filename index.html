Frequently asked questions

Answers to most common questions.

What is ProductAssist and how does it work?

ProductAssist is an AI-powered platform that helps businesses provide instant and accurate product-related support. It uses advanced language models to analyze product documentation and provide automated responses to customer queries.

How can ProductAssist benefit my business?

ProductAssist streamlines customer support by automating responses using the uploaded product documentation. It saves time, improves efficiency, and ensures consistent and accurate information delivery to customers.

How does ProductAssist handle complex or unique customer queries?

For complex queries, ProductAssist can provide suggested responses based on the uploaded product documentation. It also offers the option to escalate queries to human support representatives for personalized assistance.

Is my product documentation safe and secure with ProductAssist?

Yes, ProductAssist takes data security seriously. Your product documentation is encrypted and stored securely, ensuring the confidentiality and integrity of your information.

What is the pricing model for ProductAssist?

ProductAssist offers different subscription tiers tailored to various customer needs. The pricing is based on factors such as website integration, maximum token limits in documentation, team access, and additional requirements. Please refer to our pricing page or contact our sales team for detailed information.
